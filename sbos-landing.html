The SBOS Principle: Severity Overrides Score

A business can score Level 4 across multiple pillars but still be structurally blocked if a Critical red flag exists. A Ferrari with no wheels isn't 80% functional—it's undriveable. SBOS prevents false confidence from balanced-looking scores.
