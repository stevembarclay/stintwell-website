The Engagement Model

We don't do open-ended retainers. We engage for a specific "stint" to solve defined, high-impact problems inside your business[cite: 112, 113].

PHASE 01 // 1-2 WEEKS

Diagnostic & Audit

A rapid, deep-dive assessment of your operational health and tech stack[cite: 100]. We identify the "paper-heavy" processes and friction points limiting your valuation or scale[cite: 84].

PHASE 02 // 4-8 WEEKS

Systems Build & Cleanup

Re-architecting work flows. We implement improved tools (Zoho, Asana, QuickBooks), document SOPs, and automate handoff points to ensure structural impact[cite: 85, 86, 114].

PHASE 03 // ADVISORY

Leadership & Accountability

Aligning your team around the new operational reality. We implement KPIs, scorecards, and RACI maps to ensure visibility and continuity after the stint ends[cite: 87, 88].
